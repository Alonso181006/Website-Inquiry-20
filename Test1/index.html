Team And Know-How

We bring with us more than 30 years of international work experience and knowledge in the world of soccer. Our team is a group of professionals with the expertise to take young talent to “The Next Level”. Our management team includes people who are educated and trained in business schools (Real Madrid Graduate School, EAE Business School) that have strong ties with European premier league academies. The same sports management programs chosen by figures like Mauricio Pochettino, Manager of Premier League club Tottenham Hotspur. Among our technical staff, we have coaches who have assisted national youth soccer teams to participate in U17 – U20 World Cups. In addition, some have coached their teams to win South America National Championships. We also have coaches who have led major national teams to major Soccer World Cups.

PARTNER AND PROUD SPONSOR OF THE

“SASKGOAL UNITED PREMIER-SOCCER PROJECT”

The SaskGoal United Premier-Soccer Project will be a unique model of soccer excellence that will prepare Canada’s future national and league soccer players.

In its initial stages, this project envisions a high-performance soccer center. Ultimately, the goal is to create Canada’s First Sport Village.

SaskManagement will be one of the key partners of the project, bringing knowledge and top-tier coaching expertise to create a “Pro Soccer Network”. This network will support the development of players, coaches and managers in order to achieve an elite level in the Premier Canadian Soccer League.
