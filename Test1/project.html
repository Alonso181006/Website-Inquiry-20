Project

SASKGOAL UNITED PREMIER-SOCCER PROJECT

“Top-Talent Development of Future Premier League and World Cup Soccer Players”

SaskGoal United will be responsible not only for the development of Premier talent, but also for the development of outreach projects that will have the input of the community to let them profit the most. Soccer is a powerful instrument that can unite, engage, teach youth financial discipline, among other skills.

The future of youth soccer

We expect this project to be one of the most ambitious undertakings for the growth and development of youth soccer in Canada. In its initial stages, this project envisions a high-performance soccer center. Ultimately, the goal is to create Canada’s First Sport Village.

Due to the magnitude of this project and the social impact of it, SaskGoal United Premier-Soccer Project as a non-profit organization is developing a strategic plan to partner with other public and private organizations to join in this venture, which will revolutionize the Canadian soccer industry, putting Saskatchewan and Canada in the soccer map internationally.

SASKGOAL UNITED PREMIER-SOCCER PROJECT

Saskmanagement will take all the necessary steps to ensure the project becomes a reality. We are committed to partner with all members of the Saskatchewan and Canadian community. Together, we will play a major role in internationalizing Canadian soccer to aim for the 2026 World Cup and beyond. We foresee that by working together, Saskatchewan, in the next 10 years, can become the center of Canada’s multi-billion dollar industry.

Premier-Soccer Model

The development model will not be the same as the traditional ones found in high-performance centers. Our model will follow a holistic approach which puts the young player’s physical, mental, social and emotional capabilities at the center of its development.
